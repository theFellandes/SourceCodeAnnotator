How To

Here you can find a step-by-step guide on how to use our service.

Step 1: Upload your code

Upload your code by clicking on the "Upload" button on the navbar.

Step 2: Select your language

Choose the language of your code by clicking on the "Select Language" button.

Step 3: Select your model

Choose the model you want to use to annotate your code by clicking on the "Select Model" button.

Step 4: Annotate your code

Click on the "Annotate" button to annotate your code.

Step 5: Download your annotated code

Click on the "Download" button to download your annotated code.
